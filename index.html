Blossom makes website for you

Blossom is a CMS management system at heart and has awesome features that make organizing and managing your content a snap.

Here's how it works

1. Choose a website template.

2. Fill out content for your website.

3. Preview and publish!

After signing up users will be able to create and modify web pages and be able preview pages before publishing. At this time there is no automatic archiving of previous designs or configurations.

Uploads are permitted but limited to specific image file types. Users will be able to select a template and apply their own design preferences. Custom configurations are saved to the users profile.
